UNION OF CATHOLIC APOSTORATE (UCA)

UCA – The Union of Catholic Apostolate is a gift of the Holy Spirit, is a communion of the faithful who, united with God and with one another in accordance with the charism of St. Vincent Pallotti,promote the co-responsibility of all the , to revive Faith and rekindle charity in the church and the world, and to bring all to unity in Christ:

  • The origin, source and teacher of every apostolate for the members of the union is Jesus Christ, Apostle of the Eternal Father.
  • The call to the Union of the Catholic Apostolate is a free gift of God and Father. In his Infinite and merciful Love, he calls the person to follow his son and Apostle Jesus Christ, to continue his salvific mission in the world.

    • WHO ARE THE MEMBERS?

    The members of the union, animated by the charism of St. Vincent Pallotti consciously commit themselves to the universal apostolate of the church in every place, with all appropriate means and in collaboration with all people of good will.

    MEMBERSHIP

    - To be a member of the Union, one is required to:
  • Live out One’s Baptism as a Spiritual and apostolic vocation in confirmation with the Principal of the Catholic Faith.
  • To be fully sure with the person and the works of St. Vincent Pallotti and to be guided by His Spirituality.
  • Ready to participate in the Life and the Apostolate of the Union according to one’s state in Life and One’s responsibilities.

    • ACTIVITIES.

  • The Union of Catholic Apostolate participate in the mission of the church reawaken Faith and awareness of Vocation to the Apostolate, to rekindle charity among all the members of the people of God, and be more United in one commitment to spread charity.
  • As members of the Union, ask or beseech God’s blessing on the work of Evangelization through Prayer, sacrifice and good works.
  • To help all people to come and open up themselves to the light of Faith and to the saving power of Christ.
  • Bring the Christian message of salvation to all those who have not yet heard it.
  • Help others in their needs.

    • IMPACT FAITH AND CHRISTIAN LIFE IN THE PARISH.

  • Each disciple of Christ has the Obligation of spreading the Faith to the best of His/hers ability.
  • As Union members in the parish, our apostolate is sharing in the salvific mystery of the church.
  • By each one’s Baptism and confirmation all are appointed to this Apostolate by the Lord Himself.
  • Pray and Plan for recollection and pilgrimage for our fellow Christians.
  • Offer our service to the needy within the community and pray with them, asking for the Holy Spirit to enable them to receive and give love and renew all things.
    • All these things will happen with the help and intercession of our founder St. Vincent Pallotti and Mary Queen of the Apostle.

    RECRUIT OR HOW TO JOIN THE UNION.


    - One can become a member in one of the two ways:
  • As an individual member or,
  • As a member of the community of the Union.
