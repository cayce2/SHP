Yount Catholic Adults

youngCatholic Adults (YCA) Is the last pastoral structure dedicated to the youth in the Archdiocese,previously known as Young Adults Catholic Association(YACA).These are the youths who are aged from 27- 35yrs.It also includes those young people who are below 27 yrs but maried or with children. Y.C.A, unlike other youth groups, are rather independent because it is asumed that they are adults and capable of self-management. However,they are under the guidance of the youth chaplain in the Archdiocese, deanery and parish level.

ACTIVITIES

Parish programs
  • Seminars and workshops
  • Retreats and recolection
  • Exchange programs
  • Charitable works
    • OBJECTIVES
  • To develop a greater understanding of the Church social Teachings
  • To increase one's participation in the sacramental life of the Church and prayer-life,both communal and personal.
  • To learn,advocate,apply and uphold the principles of the Gospel and the social Teachings in the public square through Mass sensitization, walks and community service.
  • To place one's gifts,talents and energies at the disposal of the Church and the society.
  • To actively engage in the corporal and spiritual works of mercy.

    • MEMBERSHIP

  • Anyone who is between 27-35yrs
  • Anyone who is younger than 27 yrs but is maried or with a child
  • Most of the members join the group either by invitation or self-drive
