PMC (PONTIFICAL MISSIONARY CHILDREN)

This is a Society of Catholic Children whose aim is to foster their faith and missionary formation to help them take responsibility in the Church as Missionaries.

MEMBERS OF PMC
All children between earliest/Understanding Age to 14 years.

ACTIVITIES OF PMC

  • Praying general church prayers & Rosary
  • Biblical or Church teaching talks
  • Scripture readings
  • Liturgical dances
  • Games
  • Helping other children in need
  • Making small offerings for the suffering children of the World

    • IMPACTING FAITH AND CHRISTIAN LIVING IN THE CHURCH
  • Teaching them to pray 3 times a day
  • Teaching them to recite rosary always
  • Encourage them participate in Mass/Sunday School
  • Teach them to Volunteer, Charity for others
  • Respect, decent language, Happy, etc

    • RECRUITMENT
  • Inviting/Welcoming all children with a desire and ready to follow Jesus
  • Formation classes for one year
  • Enrolment to the PMC Practices
