CATHOLIC CHARISMATIC RENEWAL

This is a spiritual group within the Catholic Church which emphasizes on the power of the Holy Spirit and its Charisms to empower the lives of the Christians and up building of the Church.

MEMBERS OF CCR
All Catholic Christians of all age groups ready to find togetherness in Christ, grow in faith and living new prayer life as they encounter Jesus.

ACTIVITIES OF CCR

  • Prayer & fasting
  • Listen and teach the word of God
  • Retreat and Seminars
  • Praise & Worship
  • Guidance and Counselling
  • Intercession
  • Showing love, care and support of the abandoned, needy, etc

    • IMPACTING FAITH AND CHRISTIAN LIVING IN THE CHURCH
  • Give teachings as per the Liturgical Calendar of the Church
  • Assisting/Encouraging those not in Sacraments to join Catechetical classes and be active inChurch Sacraments
  • Encourage members attend/participate prayer meetings, Retreats, Seminars, recollections, etc
  • Encourage members attend mass always and join and participate in Church groups.

    • RECRUITMENT
  • Welcoming/ inviting Christians to participate in prayer meetings every Sunday
  • Life in the Spirit Seminar.
